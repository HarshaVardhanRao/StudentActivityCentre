About Us

The Student Activity Centre (SAC) is the hub of student life at MITS, fostering creativity, leadership, and community.

Our Mission

To provide a vibrant platform for students to explore their interests, develop new skills, and create lasting memories through diverse clubs and events.

Our Vision

To be a catalyst for holistic student development, creating a campus culture that values innovation, inclusivity, and excellence.
